The PlayStation 4 (PS4)

A home video game console developed by Sony Computer Entertainment, released on November 15, 2013, in North America, followed by Europe, Australia, and other regions shortly after.

  • DualShock 4 Controller: The PS4 introduced the DualShock 4 controller, featuring enhanced analogue sticks and triggers, a built-in touchpad, light bar with colour-changing LEDs, and a share button for easy social sharing of gameplay experiences.

  • Hardware Variants: Over the years, Sony released several hardware variants of the PS4, including the PS4 Slim and PS4 Pro. The Slim model offers a sleeker design and slightly improved power efficiency, while the Pro model boasts enhanced graphics and performance for 4K gaming and virtual reality (VR) experiences.

  • Legacy and Impact: The PlayStation 4 has been a commercial success, selling millions of units worldwide and establishing itself as one of the best-selling video game consoles of all time. It has also contributed to the growth of the gaming industry, driving innovation in gaming technology, online services, and content creation.
