The PlayStation 4 (PS4)

The PlayStation 4 (PS4), is a home video game console developed by Sony Computer Entertainment in November of 2013. Starting in North America and later rolling out to the rest of the world.

  • DualShock 4 Controller: The PS4 brought with it a new type of controller: the DualShock 4 controller. Which has sported improved analogue sticks and triggers, a built-in touchpad into the controller with a light bar that features a variety of colours of LEDs, and a ‘Share’ button for easy social sharing gaming experience.

  • Hardware Variants: Sony has unveiled various types of the PS4, such as the PS4 Slim and the PS4 Pro. The Slim model brings a sleeker design and a better power efficiency, while the Pro model enhanced its graphics and performance, catering to 4K gaming experience.

  • Legacy and Impact: The PS4 has affected the gaming world, selling millions and millions of units globally securing its place as one of the highest selling video game consoles ever made. Its impact stretches beyond sales figures, resulting in a higher influence in the gaming world, online services and content creation.
