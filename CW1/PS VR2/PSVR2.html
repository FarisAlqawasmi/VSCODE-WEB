The PlayStation VR2 (PS VR2)

The next generation of virtual gaming reality, portrayed by the PlayStation VR2 (PS VR2), brings a new level of immersion to players. By achieving this through multiple new features such as subtle performance boost, advanced 3D audio technology, new controller experience and much more.

  • Performance Boots: The PS VR2 takes advantage of the CPU and GPU found in the PlayStation 5, to ensure that every player has a smooth performance and a great VR experiences with improved graphics quality and resolution.

  • Innovative Audio: With a built-in 3D audio technology, allowing players discover new sounds from all directions. Introducing innovative audio features which can help enhance spatial awareness within virtual environments.

  • Controller Experience: This new controller gives the user an enhanced immersion and a new way of interacting with VR worlds. With new features such as adaptive triggers, finger touch detection, improved tracking, users can look forward to more realistic interactions in VR games.
