The PlayStation VR2 (PS VR2)

With the next generation of virtual gaming reality, the PlayStation VR2 (PS VR2) brings emotion and immersion to life through subtle headset vibrations, 3D audio technology and intelligent eye tracking, combined with finger touch detection, haptic feedback and adaptive triggers from the PS VR2 Sense controller.

  • Enhanced Performance: The PS VR2 is designed to leverage the advanced capabilities of the PS5, including its powerful CPU and GPU, to deliver immersive VR experiences with higher resolutions, better graphics, and smoother performance.

  • Audio Innovation: The PS VR2 is expected to offer innovative audio features to enhance spatial awareness in VR. It may come with built-in 3D audio technology, allowing players to hear sounds from all directions and distances.

  • New Controller: One of the most notable features of the PS VR2 is its new VR controller, which offers enhanced immersion and interaction in VR environments. The new controller is expected to feature improved tracking, haptic feedback, adaptive triggers, and finger touch detection, allowing for more precise and realistic interactions in VR games and experiences.
